LumenLore is a fictional company specializing in creating handmade scented candles. Our name is a compound of two words, "Lumen" and "Lore". "Lumen" means "an International Standard unit for luminous flux, equal to the amount of light emitted per second in a unit solid angle of one steradian from a uniform source of one candela" based on Google. Meanwhile according to Google, "Lore" means "a body of traditions and knowledge on a subject or held by a particular group, typically passed from person to person by word of mouth".

The company was found in 2020 by local Canadians and has been consistently growing! We uniquely design the package of our candles based on fictional stories, and give all of them a specialized QR Code that can be scanned to view fictional stories. Our products are the perfect choice for seasonal gifts for your loved ones. What are you waiting for, come check our catalogue below!
